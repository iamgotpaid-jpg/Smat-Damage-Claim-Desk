Standard Claim Process Checklist

  • Initial Report: Was the damage reported within 7 days of shifting completion?
  • Documentation: Did the customer submit proof (photos/video) within 48 hours of reporting? (If not, send 12h/24h reminders).
  • Un-invoiced Items: If no invoice, is the item a carton or a self-packed good? (Caps apply).
  • Pre-existing Damage: Does the vendor have photo proof of pre-existing damage? If not, it's assumed to be new damage.
  • Repair-First Approach: If repairable, has Urban Company been booked, or has the customer provided a GST repair estimate?
  • Finalization: Once resolved, escalate the case to the city team with the summary, penalty, and photos.
  • Missing Items: Did the vendor return the item within 48 hours? If not, proceed with refund as per invoice/cap.
